# Ayan Pal — Founder, ATSFy Technologies

> *Building revenue-generating AI products from Agartala, Tripura — Northeast India.*

## 📍 Why Northeast India

Tripura isn't a tech hub — and that's exactly the point.

ATSFy's entire brand is built on the belief that high-quality AI products don't need to originate from Bengaluru or San Francisco to be world-class. The Northeast India origin story isn't a constraint; it's a differentiator. Every product we ship is proof that geography is not a ceiling.

We represented Tripura's startup ecosystem at the **India AI Impact Summit 2026**.
